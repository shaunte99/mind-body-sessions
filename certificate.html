Why Work Across Several Sessions?

Persistent pain and tension often develop over time. A series of sessions allows space for gradual awareness, nervous system settling, recognition of patterns, and gentle reframing of emotional imprints.

This process creates conditions in which the body and mind can begin to release stored tension and shift toward more balanced responses.
